Support

Ask questions, get help, and join Premake's growing community of developers and users!

News#

For the latest news about Premake, follow @premakeapp on Twitter and our blog here on the site.

GitHub Discussions#

Visit our Discussions area on GitHub to ask questions, share your knowledge, and show off your work!

Stack Overflow#

Stack Overflow is a popular developer Q&A site and a great place for questions about scripting with Lua or Premake. Browse the existing questions tagged with premake or lua.

Contributing#

Got an idea you want to see in Premake? Great! Check out our Contributing Guidelines and feel free to send questions to the resources above. We look forward to your first pull request!

Issues & Feature requests#

We track issues and feature requests on our GitHub tracker. If you're looking for ways to contribute there are lots of ideas here! Be sure to check our Contributing Guidelines before opening any new issues.
